at the summit of Mount Corcovado, Rio de Janeiro, southeastern Brazil.
It was completed in 1931 and stands 98 feet (30 metres) tall,
its horizontally outstretched arms spanning 92 feet (28 metres).
The statue, made of reinforced concrete clad in a mosaic of thousands
of triangular soapstone tiles, sits on a square stone pedestal base
about 26 feet (8 metres) high, which itself is situated on a deck
atop the mountain�s summit. The statue is the largest Art Deco-style sculpture
in the world and is one of Rio de Janeiro�s most recognizable landmarks.
